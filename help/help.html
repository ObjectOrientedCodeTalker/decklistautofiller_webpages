Decklist Auto-Filler Guide

1. Obtain the YDKE String:

Duelingbook:

Navigate to your deck, click "Export Deck", select "YDK Code" from the drop-down menu, then click "OK".


EDOPro:

Navigate to your deck, click "YDKE", then click "Export".



2. Autofilling Decklists:

1. Navigate to the "Create Deck List" page on the registration website, and enter a title for the deck you are importing.

2. Next, open the Decklist Auto-Filler extension, paste your copied YDKE string in the box, then click "Autofill".

3. Now, wait for the extension to import the cards from your YDKE into the Decklist form. Once prompted to refresh the page, click the browser's Refresh button.
